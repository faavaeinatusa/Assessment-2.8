Community Service

Students will complete one day of community service assisting local residents and businesses. Students will be organised into small teams and given a range of different jobs from house washing to gardening, to assisting with community groups. This will usually occur within distance of Tawa College for all Year 10 students. This day has the key focus of citizenship and has been a great success in previous years
